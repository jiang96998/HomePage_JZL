Employment

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Scinece at Technical University of Munich. My main research objective is to apply robotics for various clinical applications. I joined the chair of Computer Aided Medical Procedures (CAMP) from Oct 2018, advised by Prof. Nassir Navab. Before this, I graduated from Harbin Institure of Technology (Shenzhen) in Jan 2017 and then, worked as a research assistant in Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences from Jan 2017 to Jun 2018, where I focused on the development of robot-assist spinal surgery system.
